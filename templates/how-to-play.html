How to Play Connect Game

Back to Game

Game Overview

Connect Game is a word-guessing game where players work together to guess a secret word chosen by the host. The word is revealed one character at a time through clever hints and guesses.

Player Roles

Host

- Creates the game and chooses the secret word
- Gets priority to guess when hints are given
- Cannot give hints
- Tries to guess the word before other players

Players

- Can give hints to help reveal the word
- Can guess after the host's priority window
- Take turns being the hint giver
- Work together to reveal the word

How the Game Works

  1. The host creates a game and chooses a secret word
  2. The first letter of the word is revealed to all players
  3. Players (except the host) can give hints by:
    • Providing a hint word that starts with the revealed characters
    • Writing a clue to help others guess the hint word
  4. When a hint is given:
    • The host gets 10 seconds of priority to guess the hint word
    • After the priority window, all players can guess
  5. When the hint word is correctly guessed:
    • If guessed by a player (not the host), the next letter of the secret word is revealed
    • If guessed by the host, no new letter is revealed, but the game continues

Game Rules

1. The same hint word cannot be used twice in a game

2. Hint words must start with the currently revealed characters of the secret word

3. The host cannot give hints, only guess

4. The current hint giver cannot guess their own hint

5. Players must wait for the host's priority window to end before guessing

How to Win

The game can end in three ways:

  • A player correctly guesses the secret word
  • A hint giver provides a hint word that matches the secret word
  • All characters of the secret word are revealed through correct guesses

Tips for Success

For Hint Givers

  • Choose hint words that are related to the secret word
  • Make your clues clear but not too obvious
  • Consider the revealed letters when choosing hints

For Guessers

  • Pay attention to the pattern of hints
  • Consider the context of previous guesses
  • Use the revealed letters to narrow down possibilities
